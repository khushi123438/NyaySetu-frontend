📝 How to Generate an AI Legal Document

  • Step 1: Describe Your Requirement
    In the AI prompt box, clearly write what type of legal document you want. Example: “Create a rental agreement between Ram and Shyam for 11 months in Delhi.”
  • Step 2: Include Important Details
    Mention key information such as names of parties, duration, location, payment terms, or any special conditions you want in the document.
  • Step 3: Click “Generate Document”
    Press the Generate Document button. The system will create a legal draft based on the information you provided.
  • Step 4: Download the Document
    Once the document is generated, it will automatically download as a PDF file to your device.
  • Step 5: Review Before Use
    Carefully read the document and make sure all details are correct before using it.
  • Step 6: Get Legal Approval (Recommended)
    For official or court use, it is recommended to have the document reviewed by a licensed advocate.
