How NyaySetu Works

  • Step 1: Visit the NyaySetu platform and explore available legal services.
  • Step 2: If you need a legal document, choose a template or use the AI Legal Document Generator.
  • Step 3: Enter required details such as names, location, agreement terms, or conditions.
  • Step 4: Click the Generate Document button and download the document instantly.
  • Step 5: If you need professional help, use the Find Advocate feature to search for lawyers based on your legal issue.
  • Step 6: You can also use the Chat with Advocate option to ask legal questions directly.
  • Step 7: For quick guidance, interact with the AI Legal Chatbot to understand legal procedures and rights.
  • Step 8: Stay updated with the latest laws and court updates through the Legal News section.
  • Step 9: In urgent situations, access the Emergency Legal Help feature for quick support.
  • Step 10: Always review generated documents and consult a licensed advocate before official use.
